How a TSL automation project works

We keep it simple: short call, clear problem, quick pilot, and then only continue if it clearly saves you time or makes you money.

  1. Understand your workflow — where manual work or leads are being wasted.
  2. Prototype an automation — AI agent, workflow, or micro tool within a 7–14 day pilot.
  3. Test with real data — we plug into your existing stack (WhatsApp, CRM, sheets, etc.).
  4. Decide on a retainer — if results are clear, we move to a monthly retainer or fixed plan.
